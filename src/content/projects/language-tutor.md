# Kashmiri Language Revitalization AI Tutor

## Learning Koshur for the Next Generation
As younger generations and the diaspora move away from their linguistic roots, the Kashmiri Language Revitalization AI Tutor serves as a bridge. It is an interactive, AI-powered platform designed to make learning Kashmiri accessible, fun, and effective.

### Objectives
- **Revitalization**: Encourage the daily use of Kashmiri among children and non-native speakers.
- **Pronunciation Mastery**: Use speech recognition to help learners master the unique sounds of Kashmiri.
- **Cultural Context**: Teach language through stories and cultural scenarios.

### Key Features
- **Gamified Learning**: Vocabulary games, quizzes, and rewards.
- **Speech Correction**: Real-time feedback on pronunciation using an AI model tuned to Kashmiri phonetics.
- **Story Mode**: Interactive stories based on Kashmiri folklore (e.g., "Himal and Nagrai").
- **Dialect Support**: Lessons in major dialects like Kishtwari and Poguli.

### Target Audience
- **Children**: Interactive and colorful interface.
- **Diaspora**: Connecting global Kashmiris to their mother tongue.
- **Researchers**: A resource for linguistic study.

### Impact
Language is the vessel of culture. By making Kashmiri accessible to learn, we ensure the transmission of identity, humor, and wisdom to future generations.
